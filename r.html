About TRACECOOL

Engineering the future of industrial cooling since 1969.

TRACECOOL is a privately owned, independent cooling engineering consultancy headquartered in Beirut, Lebanon. Since our foundation, we have successfully delivered over 1,000 cooling projects across more than 100 countries.

Our interdisciplinary team of mechanical engineers, thermodynamics specialists and energy consultants designs and optimises cooling systems for manufacturing plants, data centres, cold chains and large-scale infrastructure — ensuring reliability, efficiency and sustainability at every scale.

Services

Core Fields of Expertise

We deliver thermal engineering and cooling consultancy across five core sectors, combining technical precision with decades of international project experience. Full lifecycle — analysis and design through commissioning and long-term optimisation.

Request a Consultation
01

HVAC Systems

Full-lifecycle design and engineering of heating, ventilation and air-conditioning systems for industrial, commercial and institutional facilities.

02

Industrial Process Cooling

Custom cooling solutions for power generation, chemical processing, steel manufacturing and heavy industry — engineered for 24/7 reliability.

03

Data Center Cooling

Precision thermal management for IT infrastructure — server room air-flow optimisation to hyperscale liquid cooling and hot-aisle containment.

04

Cold Chain & Refrigeration

End-to-end cold storage and refrigeration for food, pharmaceuticals and logistics — designed for energy efficiency and regulatory compliance.

05

Sustainable Free Cooling

Eco-efficient cooling including geothermal exchange, evaporative free cooling, waste-heat recovery and renewable-powered systems.

How We Work

Every project follows our proven four-phase engineering methodology.

01

Site Assessment

Thermal load analysis, site surveys and feasibility studies to define project scope and performance targets.

02

System Design

Detailed engineering design, hydraulic and thermodynamic modelling, equipment selection and technical specifications.

03

Installation & Commissioning

Project management, installation supervision, system commissioning and performance verification against design targets.

04

Optimisation & Support

Ongoing performance monitoring, predictive maintenance, energy optimisation and lifecycle support contracts.
