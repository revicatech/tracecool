About TRACECOOL

Precision Climate Control Since 1999

TRACECOOL is a privately owned, independent HVAC consulting and engineering company headquartered in Worms, Germany. For over two decades, we have delivered intelligent climate engineering solutions to clients across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia.

Process

How We Work

Every TRACECOOL project follows our proven four-phase engineering methodology — on time, on spec, every time.

01

Site Assessment

Thermal load analysis, site surveys, building envelope studies, and feasibility assessments to define project scope and performance targets.

02

System Design

Detailed mechanical engineering design, hydraulic and psychrometric modelling, equipment selection, BIM co-ordination, and technical specifications.

03

Installation & Commissioning

Project management, installation supervision, system commissioning, air and water balancing, and performance verification against design targets.

04

Optimisation & Support

Ongoing IoT-based performance monitoring, AI-driven optimisation, planned preventive maintenance, and rapid-response service contracts.
